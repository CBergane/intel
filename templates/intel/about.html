About

BorealSec Intel is a read-only dashboard for public cybersecurity intelligence. It ingests allowlisted RSS/Atom feeds, normalizes and deduplicates entries, and displays sanitized summaries with transparent feed freshness.

  • Safe-by-default rendering: fetched HTML is sanitized into text before display.
  • Deterministic ingestion: each entry gets a stable id and duplicate checks.
  • Operational visibility: feed-health captures success/error and recency.
